These charities are in honour of close family members that unfortunately will not be able to share our wedding with us.

Please go here to donate

  • ARUK is dedicated to funding the best quality science and the most ambitious ideas that will take us closer to defeating dementia
  • Since their first research project in 1998, they have made advances in the following areas:
    • Understanding the causes and risk factors
    • Using transformational technology to find treatments and preventions
    • Working towards better diagnosis
  • For every £1 donated, 84p powers ARUK research and helps people to understand and fight dementia. The remaining 16p goes to fundraising and essential running costs
  • Today, five year Pancreatic Cancer survival rates in the UK are less than 7%
  • PCUK hope to achieve the following over the next five years:
    • Be there for everyone diagnosed and their families by investing a further £5 million over five years in new and improved information and services. This will include doubling the use of their national service, PCUK Support Line
    • Work with researchers, policy-makers, NHS partners and industry to launch a pioneering campaign aimed at transforming access to treatments
    • Expand and support a pancreatic cancer community with a stronger, louder voice, and collaborate with others facing similar challenges in cancers of unmet need
    • Increase funds raised year on year, aiming for £10m per year by 2022
  • Parkinson's UK vision: to find a cure and improve life for everyone affected by Parkinson's
  • Their main focuses:
    • Research - Influence and fund groundbreaking research internationally to advance understanding of Parkinson's and improve treatments
    • Information - Provide expert information on every aspect of Parkinson's
    • Friendship and Support - Offer support and opportunities to live life to the full for everyone with Parkinson's, their families and carers
    • Campaigning and Influence - Raise awareness, change perceptions and work in partnership to drive better services

Please note that any donations will be split evenly between the 3 charities.

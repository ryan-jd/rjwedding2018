Suggested lunch venues before the wedding:

Please note these are marked on the map above.

  • George and Dragon: Traditional Sandwich Pub
  • Secret Garden: Dining at The Salutation, a country retreat inside a beautiful walled garden in Sandwich (Previous owners Dom and Steph from Gogglebox)
  • Kings Arms: Classic British food at a hotel right in the heart of Sandwich
  • Hythe Bay Fish Restaurant: Modern Seafood Restaurant on the seafront in Deal
  • Le Bistro: Set above the local cheese shop called “The No Name Shop” on “No Name Street” in Sandwich, the menu serves French seasonal food
  • Hop and Huffkin: Traditional British food from a restaurant focused on sourcing locally in the heart of Sandwich
  • The Brasserie on the Bay: Situated in one of many local golf clubs, this is one of the only places within (quite a long) walking distance to the ceremony venue
  • Luigi's: A Classic Italian Restaurant, with Italian style service too

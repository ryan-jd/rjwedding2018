Getting Back On The Night

A taxi shuttle service will be provided between 11pm and 3am for use by those staying locally (in Sandwich, Deal, Worth, Ham, Walmer and Eastry).

Finding Sandilands

Please note that Sandwich Bay is a coastal private estate just outside of Sandwich town.

Coming from Sandwich town, you can use this map and/or the instructions below:

  • Take Sandown Road out of Sandwich town (Note Sandown Road becomes Guilford Road)
  • You will pass Royal St George's golf course on your left
  • When you reach the toll booth at the entrance to Sandwich Bay estate, tell the security guard that you are attending the Millwater/Dawe wedding at Sandilands
  • Pass through the toll booth and take the first left onto Kings Avenue
  • Drive to the end of Kings Avenue until you reach the beachfront and turn right (Note it will be signposted as Residents Only)
  • Keep on this road including as it turns to the right when you reach a gate to a footpath
  • Sandilands is the house on your left (Red brick)

By Train

Nearest station is Sandwich, leaves from Kings Cross St Pancras. The train station is approximately a 10 minute drive from the venue. You can find taxi details below.

By Taxi

By Car

Please note that there will be parking onsite.

Recommended route:

  • Take the M2 Eastbound towards Dover/Ramsgate/Margate and stay on this road when it becomes the A299 at Faversham
  • After passing Manston airport on your left and turning right at the roundabout (keeping on A299), take the 2nd exit at the following roundabout onto the A256 (signs towards Dover and Sandwich)
  • At the 3rd roundabout, take the 1st exit onto Ramsgate Road (Note it will not be signposted and Sandwich will be signposted as the 2nd exit)
  • This will take you through the old Pfizers estate
  • After you cross over the bridge (after traffic lights) into Sandwich town, take the left turning onto The Quay (past riverside car park on your left)
  • Follow the road round to the right and at the end turn left onto Knightrider Street
  • Keep on this road (it will become Sandown Road at the left hand bend by the church)
  • Now see Finding Sandilands above
